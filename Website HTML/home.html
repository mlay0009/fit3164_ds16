About Us


Welcome to GDC (Grocery Data Company), your trusted source for comprehensive analysis and insights into the ever-changing landscape of Australian grocery prices. At GDC, we are dedicated to providing you with accurate and up-to-date information that empowers you to make informed decisions in the face of fluctuating market dynamics.

Our team of experienced researchers and data analysts is committed to meticulously tracking and interpreting the trends shaping the cost of everyday essentials. With a mission to enhance your understanding of the factors driving price fluctuations, we delve into various grocery price influencers, from the macro outlook to grocery sector analysis.

Whether you're a consumer seeking budget-friendly solutions, a researcher investigating economic influences, or a policymaker understanding inflationary strategies, GDC is your go-to resource. Join us in unravelling the complexities of Australian grocery pricing and gain insights that matter.

Our Mission


GDC aims to focus on tracking and analyzing the rising grocery prices trend in Australia. Using advanced data collection techniques and sophisticated analytical tools, we gather vast amounts of data from a multitude of sources, including retailers and marketplaces, such as Coles and Woolworths. Our team then thoroughly process, filter, and analyze data to uncover hidden patterns, correlations, and trends.



Our Goals


  • Price Trend Analysis: Examine past price patterns to better understand the changes and factors that influence food prices.

  • Inflation Impact Assessment: Discover how inflation affects grocery prices and techniques to reduce its effects.

  • Market Segmentation: Discover varying price trends across different grocery categories to optimize purchasing decisions.
