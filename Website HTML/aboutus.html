About Us


We are a diverse team of data analysts who are passionate about transforming raw data into meaningful insights. We strive to provide clear and actionable insights that enable businesses to make informed decisions, consumers understand their shopping options, and industries to adapt to the ever-changing market dynamics. We are dedicated to providing valuable insights, analysis, and data that empower consumers, businesses, and policymakers to make informed decisions in the face of changing market dynamics.

Our Mission


GDC aims to focus on tracking and analyzing the rising grocery prices trend in Australia. Using advanced data collection techniques and sophisticated analytical tools, we gather vast amounts of data from a multitude of sources, including retailers and marketplaces, such as Coles and Woolworths. Our team then thoroughly process, filter, and analyze data to uncover hidden patterns, correlations, and trends.

Our Goals


  • Price Trend Analysis: Examine past price patterns to better understand the changes and factors that influence food prices.

  • Inflation Impact Assessment: Discover how inflation affects grocery prices and techniques to reduce its effects.

  • Market Segmentation: Discover varying price trends across different grocery categories to optimize purchasing decisions.

Methodology


GDC employs a robust and versatile data collection methodology that encompasses a wide spectrum of techniques to gather insights from various sources, including Excel and PDF sources. Our approach involves a systematic process that begins with the identification of relevant data sources. We utilize a combination of automated tools and manual review to extract, validate, and transform the data.

GDC has utilised multiple programming languages and tools in our methodology for data extraction and manipulation. We leverage a range of technologies, including specialized tools for handling PDFs, web scraping frameworks, and statistical analysis software, to ensure a comprehensive and accurate data collection process.

GDC places a strong emphasis on data quality and integrity. Extracted data undergoes thorough validation and quality control procedures to minimize errors and discrepancies. Our adaptable methodology is designed to accommodate evolving data sources and technologies, enabling us to provide users with reliable, up-to-date, and actionable insights across various domains. By employing a diverse array of techniques and tools, GDC remains committed to delivering high-quality, informative, and valuable data-driven content to our users.
